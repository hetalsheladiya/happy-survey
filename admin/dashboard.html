- Can I change Categories?

Yes! Categories are totally customizable. From the dashboard, select [Categories]. To add a new category, click the “Add new category” button and enter the category name.
Dashboard Categories Add new

- How do I preview the survey/QR code before sending it out?

Click on the [My Stores] tab to see surveys and QR codes for your stores.
Click on the store name to open the survey itself.

- Can my customer’s feedback appear on Google reviews?

Yes! If a customer leaves positive feedback, they will be shown an option to post a review on your Google Business page. Please make sure to add your Google Business URL.

- How do I add my Google Business URL?

From the dashboard, select [My Stores]. Next, select the “edit” (pencil) button and enter the link into the “Google Business URL” text box.

- Can I have multiple stores/locations?

Yes! From the dashboard, select [My Stores]. Next, select [Add new store]. For every store, a unique QR code will be generated. Print and place at your desired location where customers can scan and provide feedback. You can also use the survey links in your emails to customers.
